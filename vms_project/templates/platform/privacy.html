Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

1. Introduction

Zaryz Vistron ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Visitor Management System collects, uses, and safeguards the personal information of our clients and their visitors.

2. Data We Collect

When interacting with the Zaryz Vistron platform, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Visitor Data: Names, phone numbers, email addresses, company affiliations, and host details collected during the check-in process.
  • Security Data: Photographs, ID scans, timestamps of arrival and departure, and digital signatures.
  • System Data: Essential cookies necessary for the operation of our platform, log files, and device information for security auditing.

3. How We Use Your Data

The information we collect is strictly used for the following purposes:

  • To facilitate secure building access and maintain accurate visitor logs.
  • To ensure compliance with local security and safety regulations, including emergency evacuation procedures.
  • To notify hosts of their visitor's arrival.

4. Data Retention

We retain visitor data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law. Most visitor logs are automatically securely purged from our active databases after 30 to 90 days, depending on the client's enterprise configuration.

5. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third-party marketers. Data is only accessible to authorized administrators of the respective client and may be disclosed to law enforcement only when legally required.

6. Your Rights

Under regulations such as GDPR and CCPA, you have the right to request access to the personal data we hold about you, request corrections, or ask for complete deletion of your data from our systems. To exercise these rights, please contact the building administration where you registered, or contact us directly.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us via our Contact Page.
