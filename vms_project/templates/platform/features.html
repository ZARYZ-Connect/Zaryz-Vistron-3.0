Deep dive into
Vistron Capabilities

Explore the enterprise-grade features that power the world's most secure and efficient workplaces.

01 / REGISTRATION

Flawless First Impressions

Give your guests a premium welcome with our sleek self-service pre-registration portal. Visitors can verify their details and complete the check-in process seamlessly before they even step through the door.

  • Branded welcome screens and greetings
  • Frictionless pre-registration flows
  • Secure identity and detail verification
  • Instant host notifications upon arrival
02 / APPROVAL

One-Click Authorizations

Hosts receive comprehensive visitor approval requests containing all critical details—visit date, time slots, contact info, and purpose. Review and authorize guests instantly from any device.

  • Detailed visitor context (type, purpose, host)
  • Quick Approve / Reject decision buttons
  • Optional instructions and notes for security
  • Automated confirmation emails sent to visitors
03 / VALIDATION

Digital QR Gate Passes

Upon approval, visitors receive a dedicated digital pass with a secure QR code. This contactless badge grants them immediate access at reception or integrated security gates.

  • High-contrast, scannable QR passes
  • Personalized badges with visitor details
  • Contactless check-in and access
  • Secure validation linked directly to the VMS
04 / MONITORING

Real-Time Security Dashboard

Maintain absolute visibility over your premises. The admin dashboard tracks live statistics including total visitors, active sessions, overstays, and recent activity logs.

  • Live counts for active and departed visitors
  • Immediate alerts for visitor overstays
  • Comprehensive recent activity tracking
  • Centralized hub for all security operations
05 / ACCESS

Granular Role-Based Access

Structure your visitor management to match your organizational hierarchy. Vistron provides dedicated, purpose-built interfaces tailored to specific roles—for instance, the Security Dashboard for live gate monitoring, and the Admin Dashboard for high-level analytics, department configuration, and system settings.

  • Dedicated Admin Dashboard for comprehensive analytics
  • Focused Security Dashboard for real-time visitor tracking
  • Granular permissions and access zones for staff
  • Complete visibility control based on user roles
